Recipe Spotlight: Nourish Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup roasted sweet potato cubes
  • 1/2 cup steamed broccoli
  • 1/4 avocado, sliced
  • 2 tbsp hummus
  • Lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper

Instructions

  1. Add quinoa to a bowl as your base.
  2. Top with sweet potato, broccoli, and avocado.
  3. Add a spoon of hummus and drizzle with lemon-olive dressing.
  4. Season lightly and enjoy immediately.
